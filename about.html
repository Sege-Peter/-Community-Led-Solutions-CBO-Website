Our Background

Community-Led Solutions Community Based Organization (CLS CBO) is a registered CBO under the Ministry of Labour and Social Protection, Kisumu East Office. Formed and registered in 2020, our office is located in West Kajulu West Location, along Obwolo‚ÄìOsiepe Road, opposite Menonnite Church.

CLS CBO works with local communities in Kisumu County and Western Kenya to create sustainable solutions addressing health, governance, climate change, and child protection.

Our Philosophy

CLS is guided by the doctrine of "Nothing for us without us", ensuring authentic community involvement in development. We believe communities hold unique knowledge, resources, and social capital essential for sustainable solutions.

Our role is to inspire collective action, preserve community pride, and foster ownership of development processes. This approach acts as a catalyst for building resilient and empowered communities.

Our Vision

Resilient, resourceful, and sustainable communities.

Our Mission

To inspire and facilitate communities to create sustainable solutions in governance, health, climate change & disasters, and child protection.
